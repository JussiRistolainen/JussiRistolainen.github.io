TideMesh

Description

TideMesh is a Drop-in ocean for Unity with one-click buoyancy, dynamic tessellation, and a camera-following tiling system for infinite water. Drop it in, press Play, and your props and boats float—no scripting required.

About the Developer

I focus on simulations and optimization especially oriented towards computational fluid dynamics. I’ve built production and hobby projects in Python and C#, and I’m the creator of TideMesh.
